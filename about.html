About

The SWCTA library is about providing a place to students where they can learn, grow, and become smarter and more productive. Our library is filled to the brim with books and other resources that cover a large range of topics in order to help students learn and gain knowledge. Some of the other resources we have at the library are: a computer lab, a pay to use print system, tables in order for students to work on during lunch and after school, and much more.
